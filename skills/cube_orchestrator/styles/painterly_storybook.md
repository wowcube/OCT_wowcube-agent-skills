# Style preset: painterly_storybook

> Soft brushwork, illustrated children's-book feel. Hand-painted edges, watercolor textures, gentle gradients. Warm and inviting.

## Visual identity

- **Soft anti-aliased edges** — silhouettes feel hand-drawn, not vector-perfect.
- **Watercolor-style fills** — base color blends gently into mid-shadow, no hard banding.
- **Highlight as a lighter wash, not a bright spec.**
- **Visible "paper" or "canvas" texture** at low intensity over fills.
- **No outlines, OR very subtle pencil-sketch outline** in a warm dark hue.

## Palette policy

- 24–32 colors, warmer and more muted than other presets.
- Earth tones dominate; saturated colors used sparingly for accents.

## What this style IS for

- Cozy, narrative games. Slow-paced, contemplative.
- NOT for fast-action games where readability under motion blur is critical.

## Reference language for prompts

- "Soft watercolor fill, warm muted palette, paper texture overlay at low alpha"
- "No hard outline; silhouette defined by value contrast"
- "Hand-painted, illustrated children's-book aesthetic"
