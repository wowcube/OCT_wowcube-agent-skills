# Style preset: cartoon_thick_outline

> Bold cartoon vector look. Thick uniform black outlines, saturated solid fills, simple cel-shaded depth. Mid-density between flat and pixel-art.

## Visual identity

- **3-pixel outline**, uniform thickness, one color (deep ink, not pure black) shared across all sprites.
- **Cel-shaded fills**: base color + 1 lighter cel-shadow zone + 1 white highlight shape.
- **Light direction: top-left ~30°.**
- **Smooth curves, anti-aliased silhouette permitted** (this is NOT pixel-art).
- **Visible cel-zone borders** — the lighter cel zone has a sharp edge, not a gradient.

## Palette policy

- ≤ 24 colors total.
- Each sprite group has 3 colors (base, cel-light, cel-shadow) plus shared outline-ink and highlight-white.

## What this style IS for

- Casual mobile-game look. Approachable, friendly. Works well at 32–64px.

## Reference language for prompts

- "3px uniform deep-ink outline, cel-shaded with one light zone and one white shine highlight"
- "Smooth anti-aliased silhouette, NOT pixel-art"
